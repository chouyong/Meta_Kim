# Code of Conduct

Meta_Kim contributors are expected to keep collaboration direct, respectful, and
evidence-based.

## Expected Behavior

- Be specific about facts, uncertainty, and proof boundaries.
- Critique work and decisions, not personal identity.
- Preserve user-owned files and local environments.
- Respect security and privacy boundaries.
- Help keep discussions actionable and reviewable.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Publishing private security details, credentials, or personal data.
- Misrepresenting local checks, runtime behavior, or release readiness.
- Pressuring maintainers to bypass safety, review, or verification gates.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, restrict participation, or
escalate security-sensitive issues to private channels. Enforcement decisions
should cite the behavior, the affected boundary, and the requested corrective
action.
